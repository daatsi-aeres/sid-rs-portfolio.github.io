Siddharth Rama Sushil

Hello! I'm Siddharth, a Robotics Engineer at Addverb Technologies, India’s largest robotics company. I work at the intersection of perception, control, and deployment of industrial robots. I graduated from IIIT Jabalpur with a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering, with a focus on robotics and computer vision.

My experience spans deep learning for 3D vision, autonomous mobile robot perception, and smart industrial systems. I've interned at DRDO–IRDE working on infrared target detection and small object tracking, and also contributed to robotics research projects that won international awards.

I’m always excited by intelligent systems that integrate hardware and software to solve real-world challenges.

Research/Work Interests: Robot Perception, Deep Learning, 3D Vision, Autonomous Systems

Bachelor's Thesis: Control of Continuum Robots Using Deep Reinforcement Learning


Guides: Prof. Abhishek Gupta and Prof. Shivaram Kalyanakrishnan
[Code] 

Implemented a model-free reinforcement learning approach to train control policies for trajectory tracking of a soft continuum robot arm. Developed a custom OpenAI Gym environment and integrated it with VEGA FEM C++ middleware library and ROS to simulate more realistic dynamics.

Guides: Prof. Arpita Sinha and Prof. Leena Vachhani
[Code] 

In this work, we wish to find an optimal patrolling strategy in a multi-agent setting with the constraint of minimum information sharing. Developed patrolling techniques and analyzed their performance using ROS, TraCI and SUMO simulator
